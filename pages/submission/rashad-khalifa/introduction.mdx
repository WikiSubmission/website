# Rashad Khalifa

Dr. Rashad Khalifa, Ph.D. is key figure behind the unveiling of the Code 19 miracle along with the unifying religious message it comes with (see section [Submission](/submission)).

![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/f/f0/RashadKhalifaImage.jpg)

## Quick Timeline

<Steps>
### 1935
Dr. Rashad Khalifa is born in Kafr al-Zayat, Egypt on November 19, 1935.
 
### 1957
 
Dr. Khalifa graduates from Ain Shams University's Faculty of Agriculture in Cairo.

On October 24, 1957, Dr. Khalifa begins work for the Egyptian Ministry of Agriculture.

### 1961

Dr. Khalifa emigrates from Egypt to the United States as a student at October 11, 1961. He pursues a Master's in Biochemistry at the University of Arizonia in Tucson.
</Steps>
